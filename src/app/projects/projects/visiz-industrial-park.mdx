## Overview

**VISIZ Industrial Park** is a corporate website built to present project information, attract investors, and strengthen the digital presence of VISIZ.

The website centralizes key content such as project details, infrastructure, location advantages, and contact channels, providing a professional and accessible experience for partners and clients.

## Key Features

- **Project Showcase**: Display detailed information about industrial park projects.
- **Investor Information**: Provide legal, infrastructure, and strategic advantages.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.
- **Content Management**: Easily update content via WordPress CMS.
- **SEO Optimization**: Structured content to improve search visibility.

## Technologies Used

- **WordPress**: CMS for managing content and website structure.
- **HTML/CSS/SCSS**: Custom UI styling and layout.
- **JavaScript**: Interactive elements and enhancements.
- **Plugins**: SEO, caching, and performance optimization.

## Challenges and Learnings

The main challenge was building a complete corporate website independently while ensuring performance, SEO, and scalability. 

This project improved my ability to customize WordPress, optimize loading speed, and structure content effectively for business purposes.

## Outcome

The website has been deployed and is actively used as the official online presence of VISIZ Industrial Park, helping improve brand visibility and support investor engagement.
